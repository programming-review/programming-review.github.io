You have scriptfile located in /opt folder. You need to execute it.
There are following ways.


1) /opt/scriptfile

2) source /opt/scriptfile

3) cd /opt; ./scriptfile

4) sh /opt/scriptfile

5) cd /opt; sh scriptfile

6) . ./scriptfile
